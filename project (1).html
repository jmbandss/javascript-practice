Introduction

The Toy poodle is a dog breed that originates within North America and Germany in the 1900's. They are elegant-looking tiny dogs with a slender muzzle and long necks "Profuse and curly coat, oftenly styled by the owner of the dog. Toy Poodles have curly or wavy coats that can be soft or wiry, and come in many colors, including white, black, brown, red, cream, apricot, grey, and silver. They all grow to a specific size which is no more than 10 inches.

Traits

They are playful, agile, self-confident, and relatively calm. They are good to have if you have anxiety, or even just need a friend when you're feeling alone. Low exercise needs but they still like to go on walks and stay active. They love to be included in all family pursuits and can be good watch dogs, announcing visitors without being aggressive.

Fun facts-
